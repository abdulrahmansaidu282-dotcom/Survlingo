# AI Models

SurvLingo is designed with a flexible AI architecture that supports multiple model providers.

Potential integrations include:

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Google
- Meta
- Mistral
- Self-hosted open-source models

This approach allows SurvLingo to use the most appropriate model for each task while remaining adaptable as AI technology evolves.

The focus is on delivering accurate, secure, and construction-specific intelligence rather than depending on a single AI provider.
