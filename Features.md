# Features

## Construction Knowledge Assistant

SurvLingo provides instant assistance for construction professionals by answering industry-specific questions and explaining technical concepts.

Capabilities include:

- Quantity Surveying guidance
- Construction methods
- Material recommendations
- BOQ assistance
- Cost estimation guidance
- Engineering explanations
- Construction best practices

---

## AI Budget Guardian

Budget Guardian continuously monitors project costs against the approved client budget.

Capabilities include:

- Budget monitoring
- Cost tracking
- Budget overrun alerts
- Material cost comparison
- Alternative material recommendations
- Budget forecasting

### Example

Client Budget:

₦20,000,000

Current Cost:

₦19,200,000

Remaining Budget:

₦800,000

If a designer selects an imported chandelier costing ₦2,500,000, SurvLingo immediately warns that the project will exceed the approved budget and recommends more suitable alternatives or generates a variation request for client approval.

---

## Document Intelligence

- BOQs

Capabilities include:

- Document summaries
- Intelligent search
- Technical explanations
- Clause interpretation
